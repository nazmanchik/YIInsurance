Life

Life insurance provides financial protection for the people you care about when you pass away. Here at Y.I Manchik Insurance Agency we offer Term Life Insurance plans and policies. Term life insurance provides financial protection for a select number of years. Term insurance provides a death benefit payment if you pass away during a specified number of years. Protect your family from any financial struggles by purchasing coverage with Y.I Manchik Insurance Agency.

Business

Business liability insurance is a coverage that can protect owners from a variety of claims. As a contractor or a small business owner in Washington state, you need general liability insurance to conduct business legally. We offer both Business owners policies and General Liability policies.
