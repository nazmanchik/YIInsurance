From Seattle

  1. Follow I-5 S to S 320th St in Federal Way. Take exit 143 from I-5 S
  2. Take Weyerhaeuser Way S and S 336th St to your destination
  3. Use any lane to turn left onto S 320th St
  4. Turn right onto Weyerhaeuser Way S
  5. At the traffic circle, take the 1st exit onto S 336th St
  6. Turn right onto 6th Ave S
  7. The office will be located on the left

From Tacoma

  1. Take I-5 N to WA-18 W in Federal Way. Take exit 142B from I-5 N
  2. Merge onto WA-18 W
  3. Turn right onto 16th Ave S
  4. Use the right 2 lanes to turn right onto Pacific Hwy S
  5. Use the left 2 lanes to turn left onto S 336th St
  6. Turn right onto 6th Ave S
  7. Turn left
  8. The office will be located on the left
